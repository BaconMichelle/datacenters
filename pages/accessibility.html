For Alternate Access to Web Information

All datacenters.cio.gov information is accessible via screen readers and other accessibility tools. 

  • If you need assistance accessing any information on the datacenters.cio.gov web site, please contact us at ofcio@omb.eop.gov.
  • If you need to convert PDF documents, Adobe® offers conversion tools in its Accessibly Resource Center.
