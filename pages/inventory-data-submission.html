Inventory Data Submission

What is E-Gov Integrated Data Collection?

OMB seeks to streamline agency reporting requirements to improve meaningful use of reported data by OMB and agencies for IT strategic decision-making.

E-Gov Integrated Data Collection (IDC) is a consolidated approach to reporting requirements related to OMB's Office of E-Government and IT, the Federal CIO, and the Federal IT management community. Although established through PortfolioStat 2013, the scope of IDC and this MAX page goes beyond PortfolioStat.

Instructions and User Guides can be found at: https://community.max.gov/x/LhtGJw

Quarterly Snapshot Dates:

  • The first data collection quarter of the calendar year ends on the last day of February;
  • The second quarter ends on May 31st
  • The third quarter ends on August 31st;
  • And the fourth quarter ends on November 30th.
