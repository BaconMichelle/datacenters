Data Center Optimization Initiative Community of Practice

Agencies can participate in a forum to communicate and collaborate on their data center optimization strategies by discussing topics, including OMB reporting best practices, data center infrastructure management, and shared services marketplace participation.

The COP will hold meetings the first Wednesday of each month. GSA building in downtown Washington, D.C (the 1800 F Street location) from 10:00am until 12:00pm on.

Join the Data Center's listserv to be updated on CoP opportunities by emailing listserv@listserv.gsa.gov with no subject and "subscribe datacenters" in the body.
